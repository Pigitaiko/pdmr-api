How it works

Three steps. That's it.

🔎
01

Pick an issuer or role

Filter by company, by role (CEO, CFO, board), by buy or sell, by deal size — whatever you're watching.

📄
02

We read every filing

The moment an insider files the Allegato 3F form, we parse it into clean, structured fields.

⚡
03

Get it as data

Query it through a documented REST API, or poll the feed for everything ingested since your last check.

What this is — and isn't

These are mandatory public disclosures under Article 19 of the Market Abuse Regulation (EU 596/2014). We parse individual public filings; we don't redistribute any operator's database. This is a data feed — not investment advice, and not a brokerage or any MiFID-II-regulated service.
